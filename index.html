Eren Yeager

The hero, protagonist, and antagonist

Eren Yeager was a former member of the Survey Corps. He was the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. He lived in Shiganshina District with his parents until the fall of Wall Maria, where he impotently witnessed his mother being eaten by a Titan. This event would lead to Eren's intense hatred towards the Titans as he swore to wipe all of them off the face of the Earth to protect his friends - Mikasa and Armin.

After living a not so amusing life, these are the notable events in his life

  • Mother's death - At about the age of 8, he watched in horror as his mother got eaten by a titan.

  • Father's death - Saddened by his mother's death and father's sudden disappearance, he searched for clues, and found out he actually ate his father to inherit the AT.

  • Kissing of the queen's hands - After defending the walls (Maria, Rose, Sina), his walls came crumbling down after seeing a glimpse of the future ethat awaits the paradis devils.

  • Meeting with brother - A meeting of fate?? or one orchestrated by Eren. Here begins the plan to free the Eldians.

  • Meeting with the founding Titan - Love, hate, freedom, pain...a scene painted with mixed feelings.

  • Death - The moment Eren Yeager was finally set free from his accursed world.
